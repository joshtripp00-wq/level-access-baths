01

Zero-Entry Showers

A completely barrier-free shower entrance — no threshold, no curb, nothing to step over. The safest and most elegant option available.

Zero-entry showers eliminate the single biggest bathroom fall hazard: the step in and out. They're designed for permanent, graceful use at any age or ability level — and they look exceptional doing it.

Our solid surface panel systems create a waterproof-for-life enclosure with minimal grout lines, zero mold risk, and a clean, spa-like finish that elevates the entire bathroom.

What's Included
Flush floor entry — no threshold
Solid surface wall panels
Linear or center drain system
Lifetime leak-free warranty
02

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

Remove an old, underused bathtub and replace it with a spacious, fully functional walk-in shower — typically in just one to two days.

Most homeowners with a separate tub and shower rarely use the tub. It takes up prime bathroom real estate while creating an unnecessary daily hazard. A tub-to-shower conversion reclaims that space and replaces risk with comfort.

We handle the full scope — demolition, waterproofing, panel installation, fixtures, and cleanup. You wake up one morning with a tub problem and go to bed with a shower you love.

What's Included
Full tub removal and demo
Custom-fit shower system
Zero or low-threshold entry options
Complete cleanup — jobsite left clean

ADA & Fully Accessible Remodels

Comprehensive accessible bathroom design built to ADA guidelines — for veterans, individuals with disabilities, and anyone who needs full wheelchair or mobility-aid compatibility.

ADA-compliant bathrooms require more than grab bars. We design for proper turning radius, roll-in shower clearances, correct fixture heights, and transfer zones — all while creating a space that looks like a high-end residential bathroom, not a medical facility.

What's Included
ADA-compliant roll-in shower design
Proper clearances and turning radius
Reinforced blocking for grab bar placement
Veteran discounts available
