What Affects Your Price

The 6 Things That Move the Number

Understanding these helps you ask better questions and evaluate quotes more accurately — from us or anyone else.

Scope of Work

A grab bar installation costs far less than a full tub-to-shower conversion. The more we're removing, waterproofing, and installing, the higher the investment — but also the greater the safety and lifestyle impact.

Bathroom Size

Larger shower enclosures require more material. A compact 36"×36" shower costs meaningfully less than a 60"×36" walk-in system.

Material Selection

Solid surface panel systems come in a range of finishes and price points. We'll show you options at multiple tiers and let you decide what fits your goals and budget.

Accessibility Level

Basic safety upgrades cost less than full ADA-compliant remodels. We'll help you determine exactly what your situation calls for — not just the most expensive option.

Existing Conditions

Sometimes what's behind the wall changes the job. Rotted subfloor, outdated plumbing, or non-standard layouts can affect scope. We assess this during your in-home consultation before quoting.

Add-Ons & Features

Heated floors, frameless glass doors, built-in niches, fold-down benches — each addition has a cost. We'll walk you through what's worth it for your situation.

Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Do you require a deposit?
Yes — we typically collect a deposit before ordering materials and scheduling your installation date. The exact structure will be outlined clearly in your written quote.
Is the warranty really lifetime?
Yes. Our solid surface panel systems carry a lifetime leak-free warranty on the installation. If you ever experience a leak related to our installation, we come back and fix it. No fine print, no exceptions.
Do you offer any discounts?
We offer discounts for veterans and seniors. Just mention it when you call — we'll apply it to your quote.
Why don't you list exact prices online?
Because every bathroom is genuinely different. A number without seeing your space is either a lowball to get you in the door or a padding to protect against unknowns. We'd rather give you an exact number after a free in-home visit.
How long does installation take?
Most shower conversions and upgrades are completed in one to two days. Full accessible remodels may take longer depending on scope. We give you a specific timeline before we start.
