Frequently Asked Questions

What does a walk-in shower cost in Omaha?

Walk-in shower projects with Level Access Baths start around $6,000, with the final all-in price depending on size, layout, and the options you choose — glass doors, built-in seating, storage niches, and fixture upgrades. You get one number that covers everything from demolition to cleanup, and that number doesn't move unless you change the scope.

What's the difference between a walk-in shower and a tub-to-shower conversion?

A tub-to-shower conversion is a type of walk-in shower project — it specifically replaces an existing bathtub. 'Walk-in shower' also covers upgrading an existing dated or failing shower stall to a modern low-threshold unit. Either way, the result is the same: easy entry, solid-surface walls, and a space built around safety and style.

Are walk-in showers safe for seniors?

Significantly safer than tubs. Climbing over a 15-inch tub wall on slick porcelain is one of the most common causes of bathroom falls. A walk-in shower drops that barrier to a few inches or less, and ours include slip-resistant flooring, reinforced walls ready for grab bars, and optional built-in seating.

Can I get a glass door instead of a curtain?

Yes. Every installation includes a curtain rod standard, and glass enclosures — sliding, pivot, or frameless — are available as an upgrade in several finishes. We'll show you options during your consultation.

How long does a walk-in shower installation take?

Most walk-in shower projects are completed in days, not weeks. Our own team handles everything from demo to final cleanup, so there are no subcontractor delays.
