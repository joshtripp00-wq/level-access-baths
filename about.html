Why We Started

We Saw the Gap.
We Chose to Fill It.

Before Level Access Baths existed, our founder spent years installing accessible showers for one of the national brands. He was good at the work. He cared about the customers. But the system he operated inside didn't share those values.

The pattern was always the same. A polished salesperson would connect with a homeowner — often a senior, often a family making a difficult decision about a loved one's safety. The pitch was great. The contract was signed. And then the real experience began.

Installers who weren't properly vetted. Jobsites left messier than they were found. No one following up to make sure everything was right. Customers who spent thousands of dollars and felt forgotten the moment the truck left the driveway.

Level Access Baths was founded to do every single one of those things differently. Honest sale. Clean installation. Real follow-up. And a product that's actually built to last — backed by a lifetime leak-free warranty we stand behind personally.

We're locally owned, with no investors and no corporate quotas. Every decision we make is centered on one question: did the customer get exactly what they needed?

500+ Showers Installed Personally

Our owner has personally completed over 500 accessible shower projects — including years inside the national brand system. You're not getting a new company. You're getting deep, hands-on experience that went independent to serve you better.

The Full-Cycle Commitment

We don't just install and disappear. We check in after every job to make sure everything performed as expected — because your peace of mind doesn't end when we pack up our tools.
