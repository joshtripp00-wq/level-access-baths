What to Expect

Your In-Home Visit, Done Right

We do things differently than the national bath brands. No call center routing you to a stranger. No "today-only" pricing games. Just a knowledgeable local specialist who comes to your home, understands your needs, and gives you a straight answer.

1

You book a time that works

Pick a slot that fits your schedule. A local Accessibility Specialist — someone who knows zero-entry showers, grab bar placement, and aging-in-place design — comes to you.

2

They assess your space & needs

Your specialist measures the bathroom, looks at how you actually use it, and talks through mobility needs — including any recommendations from your occupational therapist or doctor. This is a real assessment, not a sales pitch dressed up as one.

3

You get your exact price — that day

No waiting a week for a quote. You get one honest all-in number, on the spot, covering everything: demo, materials, installation, and cleanup. If it's right for you, you can move forward that day. If not, there's zero obligation.

Our promise on pricing

Your specialist's job is to design the right solution for your home and budget — not to upsell you into things you don't need. Every quote is one all-in number with no hidden add-ons and no bait-and-switch. The price you're quoted is the price you pay.
